About DevFest

The Women Techmakers' International Women's Day campaign is a global initiative that aims to bring developers together to celebrate, connect, and learn. The campaign highlights the remarkable contributions of women worldwide and emphasizes the importance of accelerating gender equity. Throughout March and April, Women Techmakers Ambassadors and Google Developer Group communities will organize events under the theme of #ProgressNotPerfection. These events, which include technical talks, workshops, inspirational keynotes, leadership sessions, and career panels, range from large summits to intimate gatherings, ensuring that there is something for every developer.
