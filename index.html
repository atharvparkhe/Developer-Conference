About DevFest

An annual event hosted by the Google Developers Group across the world to bring technology closer to the developers. It is a All-Day developer conference where we aim to focus on multiple technologies through lightning talks, sessions, etc.
2024 will be the 13th year üéâ of DevFest which marks the beginning of the second decade of the GDG community.

Why Join Us

  • Technical content: Events hosted by the Google Developer Groups community, featuring leading experts on Google technologies in areas like Android, Firebase and so much more!
  • Roadmaps: Want to become a developer? Join us to get all the answers.
  • Panel Discussions: Learn from the interaction of an expert group of panelists.
  • Networking Sessions: Connect with fellow attendees, speakers and community leaders.
  • Job Opportunities: Connect with recruiters whose requirements match your skills.
  • Startup Opportunities: Explore the latest trends, connect with potential investors, and collaborators, and learn how to take your startup to the next level.

Rezoomex NFT Marketplace: Empowering Software Professionals in the Digital Realm

DevFest 2023 is proud to partner with Rezoomex NFT Marketplace, an innovative platform at the forefront of transforming the way software professionals showcase and monetize their skills. In the dynamic landscape of technology, Rezoomex NFT Marketplace stands out as a trailblazer, introducing a novel concept that merges the world of blockchain and resumes.

About Rezoomex NFT Marketplace:

Innovative Monetization: Rezoomex NFT Marketplace allows software professionals to monetize their capabilities by transforming their resumes into unique digital assets known as Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs). This revolutionary approach gives a new dimension to the traditional resume, providing a tangible representation of one's skills and experiences.

Blockchain Authentication: The NFTs created on Rezoomex are securely stored on a blockchain, ensuring transparency, security, and immutability. This blockchain technology enables users to prove ownership and authenticity of their resumes, setting a new standard for trust and reliability in the professional realm.

Smart Contracts Governance: The marketplace operates on smart contracts that govern transactions between individuals and companies. These contracts ensure a secure and unbiased way to transact, build reputation, and foster collaboration in the digital space.

DAO - Decentralized Autonomous Organization: Rezoomex NFT Marketplace is managed by a DAO, a Decentralized Autonomous Organization. This structure ensures a democratic and unbiased approach to searching, discovering, and engaging with professionals on the platform.

Industry Focus:

Rezoomex NFT Marketplace operates at the intersection of Technology, Information, and the Internet. With a keen focus on empowering professionals in the tech industry, Rezoomex is reshaping how talent is discovered and represented in the digital age.
